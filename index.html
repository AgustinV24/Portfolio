About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Agustin Vallejo, a game programmer and designer from Argentina. I have played games since I was a child, and some of them inspired me a lot. I want to help in the development of exciting experiences that could be as influential as the games I played in my childhood and even today.

I started my journey as a game developer back in 2021 when I entered the video game development program at Da Vinci School in Buenos Aires. I have worked on many individual and team projects, each of them helping me improve my skills in Unity Engine, Unreal Engine, and even art programs such as Autodesk Maya and Substance Painter. Additionally, I took extra courses to broaden my programming knowledge.

Currently, I am working on Astrobotanic, an isometric 3D dungeon crawler. My team and I started working on this project in Q1 2024, and we already have a playable demo that very well reflects the original idea we had.

Main Proyects

Ashes of Eric Rivel is a first-person horror game that takes place in a sinister Victorian university. The player must explore the constantly changing building and solve puzzles to uncover more information about the death of the former art teacher, Eric Rivel.
